# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Senior hiring managers, technical leads, and CTOs at product companies and remote-first teams. They open this page to verify technical seniority in under 2 minutes before deciding to reach out.

## Product Purpose

Personal portfolio for Tiago Estrela Lauer, Full-Stack Technical Lead. Success = a recruiter or hiring manager immediately understands his seniority and contacts him via email or LinkedIn.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, Precise, Senior. Technical authority without performance. The work speaks; the design stays out of the way — but "out of the way" means architectural, not invisible. Bilingual PT/EN.

## Anti-references

- Generic bootstrap dev templates (card grid, default blue accent)
- Over-animated agency portfolios (cursor trails, every section flying in)
- Resume-as-webpage (no visual identity beyond the CV content)
- Gradient text / glassmorphism overload / cream-and-sand AI defaults

## Design Principles

1. Architecture over decoration — structure communicates competence
2. Metrics over claims — numbers from real work, not adjectives
3. Restraint is active — every element earns its place
4. Dark precision — Linear/Vercel visual DNA, oxblood authority mark
5. Bilingual by default — EN/PT toggle, full quality on both languages

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion required for all animations. High contrast dark theme (near-white ink on near-black bg). Bilingual PT/EN.
